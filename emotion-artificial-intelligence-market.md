<p><ul><li>Touch-Based</li><li>Touchless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emotion Artificial Intelligence operates in two main market types: touch-based and touchless. Touch-based AI uses physical interactions, such as facial recognition or body language analysis, to assess emotional states. This approach often requires user engagement through devices, like smartphones or wearables. In contrast, touchless AI analyzes emotional cues from distance using audio or visual data, enabling emotion detection without direct interaction. Both types serve various applications, including customer service, healthcare, and entertainment, enhancing user experience by interpreting emotions effectively.</p></p>
