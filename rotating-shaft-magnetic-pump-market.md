<p><p>The rotating shaft magnetic pump market is primarily divided into single-stage and multistage types. Single-stage pumps are designed for applications requiring low to moderate pressure, efficiently transferring fluids within a single impeller stage. In contrast, multistage pumps consist of multiple impellers in series, enabling them to achieve higher pressures and flow rates, suitable for demanding applications like water supply and industrial processes. Both types leverage magnetic drive technology, reducing leakage risk and enhancing energy efficiency while maintaining reliable performance.</p></p>
