<p><p>The Automotive EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) System Market includes several key components. EGR Valves regulate the flow of exhaust gases back into the intake manifold to reduce nitrogen oxide emissions. EGR Coolers lower the temperature of the recirculated gases, enhancing efficiency and performance. EGR Sensors monitor parameters like pressure and temperature to optimize the EGR operation. The ECU (Electronic Control Unit) manages the entire EGR system, ensuring it functions properly and complies with emission regulations, playing a vital role in vehicle performance.</p></p>
