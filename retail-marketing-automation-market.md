<p><p>Retail marketing automation encompasses two primary deployment types: cloud and on-premise. Cloud deployment offers flexibility and scalability, enabling retailers to access marketing tools and data from anywhere, making it ideal for smaller businesses or those with multiple locations. Conversely, on-premise deployment involves installing software directly on company servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. This option suits larger retailers with specific compliance requirements or extensive IT resources as it can demand significant upfront investment and maintenance.</p></p>
