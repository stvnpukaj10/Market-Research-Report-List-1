<p><p>The elbow fracture system market comprises various types of fixation plates designed to stabilize different elbow fractures. The proximal radial plate is used to support fractures near the radial head, while the coronoid plate is specifically designed for coronoid process injuries. The olecranon plate is tailored for fractures of the olecranon, the bony prominence of the elbow. Other plates cater to less common fracture types or unique anatomical considerations, ensuring comprehensive treatment options for orthopedic surgeons.</p></p>
