MSG is a popular flavor enhancer that intensifies savory tastes, while HVP is derived from plant proteins to enhance umami flavors in processed foods. Yeast Extract, made from yeast by-products, contributes to a rich, savory taste and is often used in soups and sauces. Other flavor ingredients encompass a range of natural and artificial enhancers that provide diverse flavors across food categories.</p></p>
