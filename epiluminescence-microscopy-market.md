<p><p>Epiluminescence Microscopy encompasses two main types: Traditional Dermatoscopes and Digital Dermatoscopes. In contrast, Digital Dermatoscopes offer advanced features such as image capture, storage, and software analysis, facilitating telemedicine and enabling comprehensive monitoring of skin conditions over time. Both types enhance diagnostic accuracy but differ in technological complexity and application, catering to varying clinical needs and preferences within dermatology.</p></p>
