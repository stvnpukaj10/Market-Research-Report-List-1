<p><p>The Diesel Common Rail Direct Injection (CRDI) system market primarily comprises two types of injectors: solenoid type and piezo type. Solenoid type injectors utilize electromagnetic actuation to control fuel injection timing and quantity, offering reliability and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, piezo type injectors employ piezoelectric materials for faster and more precise fuel delivery, enhancing engine performance and efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in achieving optimal combustion, reducing emissions, and improving fuel economy in modern diesel engines.</p></p>
