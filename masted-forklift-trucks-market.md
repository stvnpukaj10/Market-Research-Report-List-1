<p><p>The market for masted forklift trucks is categorized into three main types: diesel, gas, and electric. Diesel forklifts are known for their robustness and are ideal for outdoor heavy-duty applications, offering high lifting capacity and long operation times. Gas forklifts, fueled by LPG or natural gas, combine flexibility and cleaner emissions, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. Electric forklifts are environmentally friendly, providing quiet and efficient operation with lower maintenance costs, making them perfect for indoor warehouse environments.</p></p>
