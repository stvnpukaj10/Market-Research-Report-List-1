<p><strong>The Handheld Vision Screeners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocular Measurement</li><li>Binocular Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Handheld Vision Screeners market is categorized into two main types: monocular measurement and binocular measurement. Monocular measurement utilizes a single eye for testing vision, allowing quick assessments of visual acuity and identifying refractive errors. In contrast, binocular measurement evaluates both eyes simultaneously, providing a comprehensive analysis of depth perception and binocular coordination. These two types cater to different screening needs, with monocular devices typically being more portable and cost-effective, while binocular devices offer more detailed insights into eye health.</p></p>
