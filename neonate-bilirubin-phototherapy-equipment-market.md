<p><p>The neonate bilirubin phototherapy equipment market comprises primarily two types: single surface phototherapy equipment and double surface phototherapy equipment. Single surface devices emit light from one side, effectively treating jaundiced infants while minimizing energy use. In contrast, double surface phototherapy equipment offers light exposure from both sides, enhancing treatment efficiency by maximizing surface area coverage. Both types are essential in managing neonatal hyperbilirubinemia, promoting faster bilirubin reduction and improving patient outcomes. Their choice depends on clinical needs and resource availability.</p></p>
