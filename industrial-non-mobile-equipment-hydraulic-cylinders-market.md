<p><ul><li>Rod</li><li>Telescopic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial non-mobile equipment hydraulic cylinders market includes two primary types: rod cylinders and telescopic cylinders. Rod cylinders are characterized by a single piston rod that extends and retracts to perform linear motion, commonly used in various machinery and construction equipment. Telescopic cylinders, on the other hand, consist of multiple nested sections, allowing for greater stroke length in a compact design, ideal for applications where space is limited, such as dump trucks and lift equipment. Both types are essential for effective hydraulic systems.</p></p>
