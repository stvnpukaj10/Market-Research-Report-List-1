<p><p>Pulsed EDFA (Erbium-Doped Fiber Amplifier) is a specialized optical amplifier utilized in fiber-optic communication networks to enhance signal strength and extend transmission distances. It operates by employing erbium-doped fibers as the gain medium, which can be excited by pumping light sources to amplify incoming signals. The pulsed configuration allows for efficient handling of high-speed data bursts, making it ideal for applications such as long-haul communication, data centers, and metropolitan area networks.</p><p>The Pulsed EDFA market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-speed data transmission and the expansion of fiber-optic infrastructure.
