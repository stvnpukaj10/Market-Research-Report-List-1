In contrast, electrical transmission converts mechanical energy to electrical energy, enhancing efficiency and control, especially in modern locomotives. Both systems play crucial roles in optimizing performance, fuel efficiency, and operational flexibility, catering to varying needs and technological advancements in the railway industry.</p></p>
