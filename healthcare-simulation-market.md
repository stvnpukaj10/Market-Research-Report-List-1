<p><p>The healthcare simulation market is primarily categorized into two types: software and anatomical models. Software encompasses digital platforms and applications designed for training healthcare professionals through virtual scenarios, procedural simulations, and patient management systems. These tools enhance decision-making and clinical skills. On the other hand, anatomical models include physical replicas of human anatomy used for hands-on training, such as mannequins and 3D-printed structures. These tangible resources allow practitioners to practice techniques and build confidence in a realistic setting.</p></p>
