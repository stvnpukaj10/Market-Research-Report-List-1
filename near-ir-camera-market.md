<p><p>The Near Infrared (NIR) camera market is primarily segmented into three types: CCD, CMOS, and others. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras are known for their high-quality imaging and low noise, making them ideal for scientific applications. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras offer faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and integration capabilities, suitable for consumer electronics and mobile devices. Other types may include specialized sensors and hybrid technologies, catering to niche applications in areas like security, medical imaging, and agriculture.</p></p>
