<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding filter market is segmented into active and passive types based on the functionality of the filters. Active filters utilize auto-darkening technology, adjusting the lens shade in real time to protect welders from harmful light and radiation, enhancing visibility and comfort. In contrast, passive filters provide a fixed level of protection, requiring users to manually switch between shades. While active filters offer more convenience and safety features, passive filters are often simpler and more affordable, catering to different user preferences and applications.</p></p>
