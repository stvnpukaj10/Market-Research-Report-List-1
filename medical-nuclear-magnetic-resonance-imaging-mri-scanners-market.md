<p><p>The medical MRI scanner market is categorized by field strength, which influences image quality and application. Low field strength MRI scanners, typically below 1.0 Tesla, are cost-effective and suited for basic imaging tasks. Mid field strength scanners, ranging from 1.0 to 3.0 Tesla, offer improved resolution and versatility, making them popular in clinical settings. High field strength MRI scanners, exceeding 3.0 Tesla, provide superior imaging capabilities essential for advanced diagnostics, research, and specialized applications, though they come at higher costs and complexity.</p></p>
