<p><p>Fiberoptic phototherapy equipment consists of two primary types: full-body and partial-body phototherapy equipment. Full-body phototherapy equipment is designed to treat the entire body, often used for widespread skin conditions like psoriasis or eczema. It typically involves a larger setup to ensure even light distribution. In contrast, partial-body phototherapy equipment focuses on specific areas, making it suitable for localized conditions. This type is more compact and is often used for targeted treatments where only a specific section of the body requires therapy.</p></p>
