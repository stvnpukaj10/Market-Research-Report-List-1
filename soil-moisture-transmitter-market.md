<p><strong>The Soil Moisture Transmitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>485 Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil moisture transmitter market is primarily categorized into two types: Analog and 485 type transmitters. Analog type transmitters provide continuous voltage or current signals proportional to soil moisture levels, facilitating straightforward compatibility with simpler control systems. In contrast, 485 type transmitters utilize the RS-485 communication protocol, allowing for longer-distance data transmission and multi-device network integration. This makes the 485 type ideal for complex agricultural or environmental monitoring applications where reliable communication between multiple sensors is essential.</p></p>
